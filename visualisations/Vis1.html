Temperature vs Latitude

When studying the relationship between temperature and distance from the equator, we found that the closer the reading was to the equator, the warmer it was. The readings were taken in May, which is approaching summertime in the northern hemisphere. We believe that this is the reason the peak to the graph (left) is slightly north of th equator.
