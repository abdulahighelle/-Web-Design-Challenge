Project Description:

We were tasked with analysing the weather of 500+ cities of varying distance from the equator. For this, we randomly selected a minimum of 500 unique cities based on longitude and latitude and performed a weather check for a specific date. From this data we selected the tempurature, humidity, cloudiness and wind speed for comparison.
